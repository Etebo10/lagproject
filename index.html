The Challenge

Lagos's Tricycle Sector
Operates in the Dark

No Verified Identity

Over 200,000 tricycle operators in Lagos without a single unified digital identity or verifiable registry.

200K+ unregistered operators

Cash Revenue Leakage

No audit trail, no accountability. Revenue collection relies entirely on human intermediaries with zero digital record.

0% digital revenue tracking

Network-Dependent Systems

Existing POS and USSD-based collection fails under network downtime — the exact conditions enforcement faces daily.

40% downtime during peak hours

Escalating Penalty Framework

Tier 1 · Warning 1 day missed

SMS warning sent to rider. Yellow flag on profile. Fund wallet same day to reverse automatically.

Tier 2 · Fine 2–3 days missed

₦500 penalty automatically debited. Profile flagged in admin dashboard. LGA supervisor notified.

Tier 3 · Suspension 4–6 days missed

QR code invalidated. Operator shows SUSPENDED on all enforcement devices across all LGAs.

Tier 4 · Blacklist 7+ days missed

Full blacklist across all 20 LGAs. Cannot operate until 100% of arrears + penalties are cleared.

Reinstatement: Pay all arrears + fines → QR reactivated within 1 hour
